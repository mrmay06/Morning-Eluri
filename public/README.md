# Custom Mascot Images

You can customize the LoverGochi companion by placing your own images here in this `/public` folder!

To use the cute custom images you uploaded in the chat, simply download them and drag/upload them to your project's `/public` folder with these exact names:

1. **`mascot_tier2_sad.png`** - The crying creature holding a broken heart (your Tier 2 image).
2. **`mascot_tier3_happy.png`** - The happy creature holding a big healthy red heart (your Tier 3 image).
3. **`mascot_tier1_gone.png`** (Optional) - The tombstone/dryness image.
4. **`mascot_tier4_evolved.png`** (Optional) - The fully evolved superstar image!

The app is fully configured to automatically detect and serve these local assets when present. If any are missing, it will gracefully fall back to the hosted CDN mascot visuals.
